About Goodview

Goodview Construction Materials & Eng. Co., Ltd. is founded in Hong Kong in the mid 90s. We are one of the major suppliers for the construction projects of a wide range of government departments including HyD, WSD, CEDD, LCSD, HKAA, ArchSD, MTR etc. We also supplied our products to private projects including the Hong Kong Disneyland, the Ocean Park, Marriot Hotel (at HKIA) etc. as well as private residential projects.

We are backed up by our factories in mainland China. We provide a large variety of construction Materials products including but not limited to large scale precast concrete products, roadwork and drainage precast products, concrete pavers, stainless steel or GMS products, natural granite stone or stone products, outdoor precast concrete furniture and flowerpots etc. Other than products of standard types and sizes, custom-made production is also available based on our clients’ designs.
